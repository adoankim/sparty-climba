Sparty Climba
Climb the mountain high as you can!

Description

Sparty climba is an endless vertical runner where you play as an spartan in a peculiar battle training.

Your duty is to climb the mountain high as you can.

In your journey you will encounter few challenges: enemies and high platforms will try to stop your progress!

Modifications

  • An simple story for the game: sparty must train in cliff climbing.
  • Intro and main title adaptation
  • New music and sounds
  • Random level generation
  • Endless vertical gameplay
  • Game UI modifications
  • New enemy: cannons
